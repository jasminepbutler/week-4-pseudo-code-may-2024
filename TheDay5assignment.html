Just a description of the socks example. N+1 is used when there were no socks. The idea is that however many pairs of sock you may have, if you pull 1 more of that number(of half the socks) you will definitely have a pair.
In the example in the image. N 2-4 are listed to represent how many socks pulled. The number across from it represents how many socks need to be pulled to have a definite 1 pair.
